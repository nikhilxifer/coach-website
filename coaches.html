Frequently Asked Questions
What actually is coaching?
Coaching is an accompanying process, a solution-oriented, empathetic companionship in different life or professional situations. The concept of coaching became known through the targeted support in sports, e.g. in tennis, where one prepared oneself specifically to overcome difficulties and wanted to achieve new goals. Coaching aims to increase the existing potential. Coaching is an interactive process between coachee (client) and coach, which usually runs over several sessions in order to consolidate a successful result in the implementation. Here, the coach is a neutral dialog partner, feedback provider in terms of solution orientation.

The intention of coaching is that a coachee can develop perspectives for himself, discover new options for action, overcome conflicts, reflect and become aware of resources, become more self-confident. Here, the coach is a conversation partner, a listener and, depending on the topic, offers methods of personality development from psychology and communication sciences. Special questioning techniques invite the coachee to reflect on the situation, the actions and to broaden perspectives.Studies describe fundamental importance to the personal relationship between coach and client for a functioning coaching process. We recommend coaches that suit you best. We only rely on coaches that we know personally and have tested.

How can coaching help me personally?
Coaching can be helpful in different situations. For example in supporting difficult professional or private decisions, conflicts or problem situations. Coaching has proven itself in supporting the achievement of long-term goals or certain changes in behavior. Other common reasons to consult a coach are stress management, career optimization, team development and self-management.
How long does a coaching session last?
In general, the duration of a session is 60 - 90 minutes, depending on the complexity of the topic, the course and the respective target agreement.
Is my data protected (do my conversations remain confidential)?
All topics and concerns that you discuss with your coach remain between you and are treated confidentially. The coaches have a duty of confidentiality. comcare does not learn anything about the content of the coaching sessions either.
